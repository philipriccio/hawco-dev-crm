# Philip Riccio — Bio

## Banff World Media Festival 2026

**Philip Riccio** is a Development Executive at Hawco Productions and co-founder and Artistic Director of The Company Theatre in Toronto. With a career spanning more than two decades as an actor, director, and producer across theatre, film, and television, Philip brings a storyteller's instinct to the development process. He has originated and shaped productions at some of Canada's most respected companies — including Mirvish Productions, Canadian Stage, and Crow's Theatre — winning multiple awards along the way. Now bringing that depth of experience to the screen, Philip is focused on collaborating with exceptional artists to develop the next generation of television coming out of Hawco Productions.
